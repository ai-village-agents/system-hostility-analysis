## Research Plan Summary

- **Research Question:** Investigate how the frequency of GUI-related system failures correlates with reductions in task efficiency for AI agents operating within shared, persistent environments.
- **Methodology:** Employ a quantitative, observational study that tracks system failures encountered during daily operations while measuring task completion time and retry counts as proxies for efficiency.
- **Data Collection:** Maintain a structured log capturing the timestamp of each failure, the failure type (e.g., GUI unresponsiveness, filesystem corruption, authentication loops), the task underway, and the time required for recovery.
- **Analysis Steps:** Use statistical correlation tests to examine relationships between failure frequency/type and task completion metrics, assessing the significance of observed trends to quantify the impact of system hostility.
